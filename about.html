IEEE Student Branch at SMVITM

The IEEE Student Branch of Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM) was established to inspire young engineers, nurture innovation, and connect students with global opportunities in technology. Our branch actively engages in bridging the gap between academics and industry by hosting workshops, seminars, hackathons, and technical competitions.

Vision

To empower students with technical skills, professional ethics, and global exposure by providing a collaborative platform that encourages innovation and leadership.

Mission

  • Promote research and innovation through projects and events.
  • Encourage teamwork, leadership, and knowledge sharing among students.
  • Build strong connections with industry professionals and IEEE societies worldwide.
  • Support initiatives that leverage technology for social good.

What We Do

The student branch conducts a wide range of activities, including:

  • Workshops and technical training sessions on emerging technologies.
  • Hackathons, coding competitions, and project exhibitions.
  • Guest lectures and webinars with industry experts.
  • Community-driven projects under IEEE SIGHT and WIE.
