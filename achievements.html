Achievements

Major Highlights

  • IEEE STEM Champion:

    Annapurna Shenoy, a third-year Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) student, was named an IEEE STEM Champion. She was one of only 28 volunteers chosen globally for this prestigious title, which recognizes her significant contributions to pre-university STEM education.

  • Vidhyanidhee IEEE Bangalore Section Scholarship 2025:

    Sujan Kumar K, a third-year Computer Science Engineering (CSE) student, was awarded this ₹10,000 scholarship. The award recognizes his academic excellence and active involvement in IEEE initiatives.

  • IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) Scholarship (2024–25):

    Five female students from SMVITM were awarded this scholarship, receiving ₹50,000 each. The award was funded by Quest Global and facilitated by IEEE India Philanthropy (IIP).
